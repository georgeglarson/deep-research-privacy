About Deep Research

A mission to make AI research both powerful and private

Technical Innovation

Deep Research represents a new approach to AI research tools, combining advanced language models with robust privacy protections. Built with modern TypeScript and following clean architecture principles, it demonstrates how powerful AI capabilities can coexist with strong privacy guarantees.

Architecture

Clean, maintainable TypeScript codebase with comprehensive testing and documentation. Modular design enables easy integration of new AI models and search providers.

Privacy Engineering

Innovative local processing techniques and zero-logging architecture ensure research data never leaves your control. Built-in rate limiting and error recovery maintain reliability.

Integration Design

Seamless integration of Venice.ai's advanced models with Brave's private search, demonstrating practical privacy-preserving AI applications.

Open Source Collaboration

This project is maintained by George Larson as part of ongoing research into privacy-preserving AI systems. Contributions and feedback are welcome.
